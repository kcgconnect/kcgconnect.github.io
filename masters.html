Embark on Your Educational Journey:
KCG Connect+ Master's Fair Pass Application

Are you ready to explore new horizons in your academic journey? KCG Connect+ is thrilled to present the Master's Fair in Chennai, a gateway to discover opportunities, insights, and possibilities in higher education.

About the Event:

Join us on December 14, 2023, from 17:30 to 21:00 at Hyatt Regency Chennai for an evening filled with networking, live seminars, and the chance to interact with representatives from 40+ top universities globally. From free master's info sessions to the application support and the opportunity to apply for QS ImpACT scholarships, this event is a comprehensive package for those aspiring to pursue master's education.

Application Process:

To ensure your spot at this exclusive event, fill in the KCG Connect+ Master's Fair Pass Application form. Provide us with your details, and you'll receive your ticket to a world of educational possibilities.

Why Attend?

‣ Network with global universities.
‣ Gain expert advice on admissions.
‣ Apply for QS ImpACT scholarships.
‣ Free counseling and CV review.
